A place I'd like to visit

If I could go anywhere in the world, I would go to Giant's Causeway in Ireland because I think it's the magical place in the world. The architecture of the rocks is so natural and yet so unnatural. The hexagonal shapes are unlike anything I've ever experienced first-hand. I have seen honeycombs before, but those are made generally quickly and uniformly, whereas rocks are rarely ever uniform and almost never in that size. In general, I just want to visit the most magical places
